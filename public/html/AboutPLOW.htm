About Plow

Plow Publishing is an active open access publisher specializing in the publication of academic journals across of science, technology and medicine. It is known for its diverse and innovative publication approaches in promoting and expediting scientific research.


Ethics, Policies and Instructions

Plow Publishing Company strives to ensure all of its published journals follow the best practice guidance from leading organizations around the world in academic publishing. All journals published by Plow are members of Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE)
