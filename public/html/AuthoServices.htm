Author Services

   Our author services are fast, accurate, and competitively priced. All manuscripts are welcome to use our services no matter if they aim for Plow journals or not

English Editing Services

   Apart from grammar and spelling, Plow also provides in-depth English language modification services which include logical flow, smoothness and consistency of terminology. Papers of all subjects will be modified by two native English-speaking experts who will be responsible for a final high-quality manuscript.

   When reviewing the draft, English editors examine lexical selection, sentence structure and clear language expression based on the academic style and expression usage of native English speakers. They also maintain communication with you to make sure modification suggestions perfectly with your expectations.

   When you choose Plow for English language modification, we will also provide free formatting.
